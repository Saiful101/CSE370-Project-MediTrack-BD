# MediTrack BD
A Smart Healthcare Management System for Bangladesh


## Project Idea
In Bangladesh, patients lose their medical history, waste time in long queues at doctors, face risks from taking conflicting medicines together, and struggle to find blood donors in emergencies.

MediTrack BD is an integrated platform where patients, doctors, and pharmacies are all connected — managing everything from appointments to prescriptions, medicine inventory, and blood bank in one place.

## Features
1️⃣ Smart Appointment Booking System

How it works:
	∙	Patient logs into the system with their name, phone number, and desired date
	∙	System displays a list of all available doctors along with their free time slots
	∙	Patient selects a preferred doctor and time slot to confirm the appointment
	∙	If the selected slot is already booked, the system automatically suggests the next available time
	∙	Patient can cancel or reschedule their appointment at any time
	∙	Both the doctor and patient receive a confirmation notification
  
2️⃣ Digital Prescription & Drug Interaction Warning

How it works:
	∙	After examining the patient, the doctor logs into the system and creates a digital prescription
	∙	The prescription includes diagnosis, medicine names, dosage, frequency, and duration
	∙	As each medicine is added, the system automatically checks the Drug Interaction Database
	∙	If two medicines are dangerous to take together, the system immediately shows a warning alert
	∙	The warning displays a severity level — Low / Medium / High
	∙	Once finalized, the patient can download the prescription as a PDF
	∙	The prescription is automatically sent to the pharmacy

3️⃣ Patient Medical History Tracker

How it works:
	∙	Every patient has a unique profile where all their information is stored
	∙	The profile includes — blood group, allergy list, chronic diseases, and past diagnoses
	∙	After every doctor visit, the new prescription and diagnosis are automatically added to the history
	∙	Any authorized doctor can log in and view the patient’s complete medical history
	∙	The patient can also view and download their own history at any time
	∙	This eliminates the need to repeat tests or explain past conditions every visit

4️⃣ Pharmacy & Medicine Inventory System
How it works:
	∙	Each medicine’s stock quantity, price, and expiry date are entered into the system
	∙	As soon as a doctor sends a prescription, the pharmacy automatically receives a notification
	∙	When a medicine is sold, the stock quantity is automatically reduced
	∙	If any medicine’s stock falls below the reorder level, the system sends an alert
	∙	If a medicine’s expiry date is approaching, the system gives an early warning
	∙	Daily sales records are automatically saved in the system

5️⃣ Blood Bank & Donor Management

How it works:
	∙	Blood donors register in the system with their name, blood group, phone number, and address
	∙	When someone needs blood, they search by blood group and get a list of eligible donors
	∙	The system automatically checks when the donor last donated blood
	∙	If 3 months have not passed since the last donation, the donor is marked as ineligible
	∙	Contact information of eligible donors is visible so they can be reached directly
	∙	Donors can also view their own donation history

6️⃣ Health Analytics & Report Dashboard

How it works:
	∙	When admin logs in, a dashboard displays all important data in one place
	∙	Dashboard shows — total patients, total appointments, daily/monthly revenue
	∙	A trend chart shows which diseases are being diagnosed most frequently
	∙	Doctor-wise patient count is visible — showing how many patients each doctor has seen
	∙	Monthly pharmacy sales reports and most sold medicines list are available
	∙	Monthly blood donation statistics are displayed
	∙	All reports can be filtered by date range, doctor, or department
